March 2026

PIVAI Takes SignMap to the Field in Newberry, SC

In March 2026, PIVAI conducted a hands-on field demonstration in Newberry, South Carolina, putting our AI-powered inspection platform to the test on real roads alongside SCDOT officials and local infrastructure managers.

The demo showcased SignMap's real-time pavement defect detection — identifying potholes, cracking, and surface deterioration as the vehicle drove through Newberry's road network — as well as automated traffic sign recognition.

Attendees were able to observe live data collection, on-device AI processing, and the interactive mapping dashboard that aggregates field data into actionable maintenance reports.

This demonstration marks an important milestone in PIVAI's mission to bring affordable, scalable AI inspection tools to municipalities and state DOTs across the Southeast.
