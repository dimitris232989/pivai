South Carolina Transportation Forum

March 31 – April 1, 2026

Meet PIVAI at the 2026 South Carolina Transportation Forum

PIVAI LLC will be exhibiting at the 2026 South Carolina Transportation Forum, held March 31–April 1 at the Columbia Metropolitan Convention Center. We invite transportation professionals, engineers, and infrastructure managers to visit our booth and learn how PIVAI’s AI-powered inspection platform helps agencies efficiently detect and map pavement defects such as potholes, cracks, and signage.

Our technology combines advanced computer vision with vehicle-mounted sensors to automate roadway condition assessment and support data-driven maintenance decisions.

Stop by our booth to see how PIVAI is helping modernize infrastructure inspection across South Carolina.
